Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. It tastes good.

It is also vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Can of Tomatoes
  • 1 Bag Spinach
  • Lots of Ricotta

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Put Noodles in a pan
  4. Layer tomato, spinach, ricotta, between noodles
