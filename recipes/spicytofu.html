Spicy Tofu

Description

This is a recipe for spicy tofu. It is pretty salty, but it's tasty.

It is also vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of silken tofu
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • Szechuan Peppercorns
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Heat the soy sauce
  2. Add the peppercorns
  3. Cook the rice
  4. Pour the sauce on the tofu
